About

A farmer assistance portal project helps farmers ensure higher profitability through direct communication. This Farmer Assistance Portal Project boosts business communication and brings transparency to the system.
This Farmer Assistance Portal Project allows for the excellent farmer, retailer, and supplier communication. It allows farmers to login and communicates to respective dealers.
The farmers can also submit their grievances to respective authorities or dealers using their farmer login on a separate complaints page. The authorities will get access to that page regularly using their login id and passwords.
